This file consist of the contents used for making the ice_tea. 
Add 5.25 cups (approximately 1.25 litres) of water to a saucepan.
Set the pan over a medium heat and bring the water come to a boil.
Turn off the heat once the water comes to a boil. Immediately add 1 to 2 tablespoons of black tea powder, depending on how strong you want your tea to taste.

Alternatively, you can use black tea leaves or green tea leaves.

adding black tea powder to boiling water.
Add ⅓ to ½ cup raw sugar or granulated white sugar. For a less sweet taste, you can add ¼ cup sugar.
Mix very well and let the tea leaves steep in the water for 4 to 5 minutes. For a lighter tea flavor, you can steep for 2 to 3 minutes.
 After the tea has steeped in the hot water, then add 3 tablespoons of lemon juice to the pot. For a stronger lemony taste, you can add 4 tablespoons of lemon juice.
 Stir well with a spoon.
